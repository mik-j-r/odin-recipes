Classic Chicken Curry (with Herby Rice & Flaked Almonds)

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken stock pot
  • 150g white basmati rice
  • 1 onion, 1cm dice
  • 3 tbsp fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 150g green beans, trimmed and cut into 3 pieces
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, peeled and very finely chopped
  • 2 skinless chicken breasts, chopped into 2cm dice
  • 3 tbsp flaked almonds
  • flavourless oil
  • 1.5 tsp curry powder
  • 4 tbsp mango chutney
  • 50g raisins
  • 150ml double cream
  • salt and black pepper
